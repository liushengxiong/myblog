## An Ordinary Person's Response to the AI Era

In early 2023, when I first used ChatGPT, I realized a new era had arrived.

As a marketing and operations professional with over a decade of experience, I've witnessed the fierce competition in FMCG, the rapid growth of the internet industry, and the rise of B2B e-commerce. But no technological shift has made me feel both excited and anxious like AI.

Excited because AI provides individuals with unprecedented leverage. Anxious because if we don't actively embrace change, we risk being left behind.

## Why Now?

The idea of building this website had been brewing for a long time, but I only recently took action. Three key factors pushed me forward:

### 1. AI Has Lowered the Barrier to Content Creation

In the past, building a professional personal website required technical expertise or hiring developers. Now, with AI tools, I can quickly build, iterate, and optimize this site—from design to code, from content to SEO, AI helps at every step.

This made me deeply realize: **AI doesn't replace humans; it amplifies human capability**.

### 2. Personal Brand Is Becoming the Most Important Asset

In an age of information overload, attention is the scarcest resource. And personal brand is the best way to capture it.

I've observed that those who consistently create value online are gaining more opportunities:

- More career choices
- Greater bargaining power
- More meaningful connections
- Larger influence

As Naval Ravikant says: "Productize yourself." This is one of the most important survival strategies in the AI era.

### 3. Writing Is the Best Way to Think

I've always believed that writing is thinking refined. When you try to transform vague ideas into clear words, you're actually engaging in deep thinking.

This website forces me to:

- **Systematize knowledge**: Organize scattered experiences into coherent frameworks
- **Make public commitments**: Use public writing to hold myself accountable to continuous learning
- **Build connections**: Attract like-minded people and form learning communities

## What This Website Is About

This is not a tech blog, nor a business analysis newsletter. It's a **personal growth laboratory**—documenting my journey of exploring personal growth, business models, and content creation in the age of AI.

Specifically, I'll share:

**AI Tool Applications**
- How to use AI to boost work efficiency
- How to build personal AI workflows
- How to evaluate and choose AI tools

**Personal Growth**
- Frameworks for cognitive upgrades
- Time and energy management
- Learning strategies and knowledge management

**Business Models**
- Exploring the "company of one" model
- Content entrepreneurship practices
- Building personal business systems

**Content Creation**
- Writing methodologies
- Content strategy and distribution
- Personal brand building

## My Commitments

I commit to:

1. **Authentic documentation**: Sharing real successes and failures, no packaging
2. **Consistent output**: Maintaining a steady publishing rhythm, not chasing viral hits
3. **Deep thinking**: Every article is carefully considered, no information garbage
4. **Open connection**: Welcoming exchanges and feedback, growing together
